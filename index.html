About Us & Our Story

We are a non-profit dedicated to providing resources, technology, and educational opportunities to children in underserved communities with no access to schooling or the internet.

Teckies was founded to address a digital divide affecting millions of children across Africa. Many lack access to the internet, modern education tools, and basic digital literacy. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to thrive in a connected world. We want to empower 1,000+ kids with access to technology by 2026. Build community learning centers in underserved areas. Provide digital literacy training for youth and educators.

We envision a future where every child, no matter where they live, has equal access to quality education and digital tools, enabling them to participate fully in our global digital economy.
